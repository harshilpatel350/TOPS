All notable changes to **Generative AI Lab** are documented here.

The format follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/),

## [1.0.0] — 2025-07-01

### Added

- **Session 01** — Introduction to Generative Models (theory, pandas tables,
  prompt-engineering examples with ChatGPT-style Q&A).
- **Session 02** — Autoencoders & VAEs on Fashion-MNIST (MSE, BCE, denoising,
  latent sampling, loss plots, generated images).
- **Session 03** — Generative Adversarial Networks (simple generator,
  discriminator class, adversarial loop simulation, research comparison).
- **Session 04** — Diffusion Models (forward diffusion, reverse approximation,
  noise visualization, saved generated images).
- **Session 05** — Industry Applications (IPL summarizer, restaurant chatbot,
  playlist filter, prompt engineering, challenge analysis).
- **genai_intro.ipynb** — Standalone gentle introduction to Generative AI.
- **comparison_traditional_ai_vs_genai.ipynb** — Side-by-side comparison
  notebook with pandas tables and visualizations.
- Reusable `src/` modules: data loading, autoencoders, GANs, diffusion,
  chatbot, summarizer, playlist filter, prompt engineering, utilities.
- Pytest test suite for utilities and chatbot.
- Full documentation: README, CONTRIBUTING, LICENSE (MIT).
